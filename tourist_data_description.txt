The dataset contains the several information of the 250 tourists coming to 
the Antalya during the summer 2022. 

gender: the gender of the tourists
country: the country of the tourists who visited Antalya
age: the age of the tourists 
education_level: the education level of the tourists 
monthly_income: the salary of the tourist in the month (USD)
spending_money: the amount of money that the tourist spent during the vacation (USD)
height: the height of the tourist
weight: the weight of the tourist 
number_of_stay: the duration of the tourist being in Antalya(day)
marital_status: the marital status of the tourist (yes: married, no: not married)
